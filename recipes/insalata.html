Insalata recipe:

Ingredients:

  • cups of mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup of cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by washing and drying your salad greens
  2. them in a large bowl.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and Kalamata olives to the bowl.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and balsamic vinegar to make the dressing.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss everything together until evenly coated.
  6. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top of the salad.
  7. Season with salt and pepper
  8. Enjoy!
