Ragù recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup dry red wine
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, carrot, and celery, and cook until softened.
  2. Add ground beef and pork, and cook until browned, breaking up the meat with a wooden spoon as it cooks.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine, beef broth, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, for at least 2 hours (or up to 4 hours) until the sauce has thickened and the flavors have melded together.
  5. Remove the bay leaf before serving the ragù over pasta or polenta.
